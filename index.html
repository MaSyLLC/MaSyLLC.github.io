How to Prepare Your Device

Please follow these steps *before* you ship. We cannot buy devices that are locked to a personal account.

1. Back Up Your Data

Save your photos, files, and contacts to the cloud or an external drive. We will wipe all data permanently.

2. Sign Out of All Accounts

This is the most important step! Please sign out of your **iCloud (Apple ID), Google, or Samsung account**.

3. Factory Reset

After signing out, go to your settings and perform a full factory reset to erase your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine your offer?

Our offers are based on the device model, its cosmetic and functional condition, and current market value. We're a team of tech experts, and we aim to give the most competitive, fair price possible.

What happens if my device isn't as described?

This is our "Gotcha" policy: there is no gotcha. If we receive your device and its condition doesn't match your description, we'll email you a revised, fair offer. If you decline, we ship your device right back to you, 100% free of charge.

Is my personal data safe?

Absolutely. We professionally wipe all data from every device we receive as a standard part of our process. We'll also guide you on how to back up and wipe your device before you even ship it.

How long does it take to get paid?

Once we receive your device, we inspect it within 1-2 business days. As soon as inspection is complete and matches your description, we send your payment immediately via PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle.
