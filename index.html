Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine your offer?

Our offers are based on the device model, its cosmetic condition, and its functional condition, as well as current market value. We aim to give the most competitive, fair price possible.

What happens if my device isn't as described?

This is our "Gotcha" policy: there is no gotcha. If we receive your device and its condition doesn't match your description, we'll email you a revised, fair offer. If you decline, we ship your device right back to you, 100% free of charge.

Is my personal data safe?

Absolutely. We professionally wipe all data from every device we receive as a standard part of our process. You can be confident your personal information is securely destroyed.

How long does it take to get paid?

Once we receive your device, we inspect it within 1-2 business days. As soon as inspection is complete and matches your description, we send your payment immediately via PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle.
