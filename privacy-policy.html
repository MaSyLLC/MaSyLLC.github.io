MaSy LLC Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

MaSy LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services to sell your used electronics.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a cash offer and shipping services, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing Address (State and/or Full Address for shipping labels)
  • Device Details (Brand, Model, Serial Number, Specs)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • Quotes: To calculate the value of your device and send you a cash offer.
  • Shipping: To generate prepaid shipping labels via our carrier partners.
  • Payment: To issue payments to you via PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle.
  • Communication: To contact you regarding the status of your order or offer.

4. Data Security & Device Wiping

  • Personal Data: We implement security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
  • Device Data: Upon receipt of any device (laptop, PC, tablet, etc.), MaSy LLC performs a secure data wipe to permanently erase all previous user data.
